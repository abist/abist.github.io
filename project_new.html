Parallel Seam Carving  

Seam Carving is a content-aware image resizing algorithm developed by Shai Avidan, of Mitsubishi Electric Research Laboratories (MERL), and Ariel Shamir, of the Interdisciplinary Center and MERL. Created optimized implementations of image seam-carving on both NVIDIA GPU and multi-core x86 CPU platforms and came up with an algorithm which can switch between content aware (seam carving) and content agnostic (bilinear interpolation) retargeting after inspecting some global statistics of the image.
